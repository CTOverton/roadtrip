Sault Ste. Marie → Bruce Peninsula

≈ 5 hr 40 drive + Chi-Cheemaun ferry
Wake7:30 AM
Depart8:15 AM
Arrive~7:00 PM
DifficultyLong
Drive (Google): ~5h40 (split by ferry) Ferry: ~1h45 peak / 2h off-peak Time zone: All Eastern
~4h15 from Sault Ste. Marie down to South Baymouth on Manitoulin Island, then the Chi-Cheemaun ferry across to Tobermory (~1h45 peak season / 2h off-peak). The ferry runs a fixed schedule with limited daily sailings — book a vehicle reservation ahead and plan to arrive ~45–60 min before departure. Tobermory drops you right at Bruce Peninsula NP / the Grotto; from there it's ~1h25 south to Port Elgin. Final arrival depends on which sailing you catch.
